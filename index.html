About Me

Tu Nguyen

Hello! My name is Tu Nguyen, my pronouns are he/him and I'm a undergraduate student at UW Bothell. I am studying and majoring in Interactive Media Design. I graduated from Ballard High School and enjoys doing activities outside. I wanted to pursued in the field of IMD UX because I enjoy every moment of designing. I was inspire by Paula Scher quote, of I interpreted her quote as failure is the fuel that powers and give motivations to improve on. Whenever I feel like stuck by an overwhelming challenge, this quote helps motivate my determination to succeed.
